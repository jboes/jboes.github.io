Generating a systematic chemical network

Published: Wed 01 November 2017
By Jacob

In blog.

tags: database graph-theory

I've been interested in database design for a while now for the purpose of collecting catalytic information in an intelligent and reusable fashion. There are several examples of this already being done in the field of materials science. Some examples of this include:

  • Materials Project
  • Open Quantum Molecular Database (OQMD)
  • AFLOW

Each of these databases does a good job keeping track of the information needed to reproduce the quantum energy calculations which are performed to obtain their associated energies.
